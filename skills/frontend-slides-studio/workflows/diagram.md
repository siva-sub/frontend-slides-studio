<workflow>
1. Choose the diagram type from the relationship being explained, not from aesthetics.
2. Prefer a table or paragraph if it teaches the same thing more clearly.
3. Keep one semantic node/edge idea per stable ID. Use at most two focal nodes and stay within the type's complexity budget.
4. Validate DiagramSpec before rendering. Off-axis edges must be rounded orthogonal routes; shared ports fan; obstacles are avoided; labels sit 6–10px away on masks.
5. Render inline SVG for HTML. Bind optional motion to node/edge IDs. Reduced motion must show the complete diagram.
6. For editable PPTX, use the DiagramSpec adapter rather than rasterizing the whole diagram.
</workflow>
